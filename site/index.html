OpenShortcuts

Curated iOS Shortcuts that connect your iPhone to AI, speech-to-text, and productivity APIs.

Open Source Provider Agnostic Privacy Focused

How to Install

1

Tap Install

Opens the Shortcuts app on your iPhone or iPad

2

Review & Add

iOS shows you what the shortcut does before adding

3

Configure

Enter your API key and endpoint when prompted

4

Use It

Tap from Home Screen, widget, Share Sheet, or Siri

Requires iOS 16+ and "Allow Untrusted Shortcuts" enabled in Settings > Shortcuts. You'll also need an API key from your chosen provider (OpenAI, Groq, Anthropic, etc.).
